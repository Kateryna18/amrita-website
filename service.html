Decontracting body massage

  • Effect within 24-48 hours

    After a massage, you will feel the effect of calmness and relaxation last for a long time, giving you a pleasant feeling of comfort and coziness even after the session is over.

  • Deep relaxation

    Massage will help you release accumulated tension and stress, and feel deeply relaxed.

  • Relieving muscle tension

    Massage will help you to understand your muscles and reduce pain or discomfort associated with muscle tightness.

About procedure

Relive the feeling of deep relaxation and balance your inner state with our Body Relaxation Massage service. Our expert massage therapists will help you release tension and fatigue by working with healthy techniques and inspiration.

What else

The beneficial effect of manual massage on the human body is difficult to overestimate. Nowadays, massage is one of the means of recovery. Due to its effectiveness, massage helps to quickly recover from stress, relax tense muscles and restore a sense of calm and well-being. Real masters of their craft, high-quality cosmetic materials, a cozy and warm atmosphere will give you complete relaxation and well-being.

For whom it is intended

Anyone who feels tired from a busy lifestyle. And also has a desire to get rid of the visible manifestations of cellulite, feels pain in the muscles.

Who is not suitable for

Pregnant women, only with the consent of a doctor.

  • Duration

    50 - 60 minutes

  • Techniques

    Gentle, relaxing movements and stretching of the muscles.

  • Procedure

    The massage is performed on a special bed using natural oils or creams. The masseur uses gentle and relaxing movements aimed at relieving muscle tension and improving blood circulation.

  • Benefits

    Improved sleep quality, reduced muscle pain and discomfort, improved emotional state, increased muscle flexibility.

  • Recommendations

    It is recommended to drink water before and after the massage to maintain hydration. It is usually recommended to repeat the procedure once every 1-2 weeks for best results.

  • Contraindications

    Massage may not be recommended for certain medical conditions, such as varicose veins, thrombosis, acute trauma, inflammation of the skin, etc. It is recommended to consult a doctor before having a massage.
